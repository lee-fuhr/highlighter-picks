Quick answer

The Zebra Mildliner Pastel Color Set of 5 is our top pick for the truest, most soothing pastel highlights that won’t overpower your notes.

The Zebra Mildliner Double-Ended Set offers unmatched versatility for everyday highlighting, while the Pilot FriXion Light Erasable Set is the go-to for anyone who wants the freedom to correct mistakes.

Pastel highlighters bring a welcome softness to note-taking, planning, and studying. Unlike their neon counterparts, these muted tones reduce visual strain and let your written words stay readable, making them a favorite among bullet journalers, students, and anyone who spends long hours with paper. Their gentle colors also work beautifully on cream or off-white paper, adding a touch of calm to an otherwise busy page. When picking a set, consider tip style: chisel tips cover wider lines, while fine tips suit underlining. Color range matters, some sets offer a handful of curated pastels, others a full spectrum. Erasability is a bonus if you revise frequently, but it often comes with a trade-off in vibrancy. Finally, think about bleed-through and smear resistance; well-regarded brands reliably minimize both.
