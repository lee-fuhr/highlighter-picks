How we choose our picks

We do not run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, we rely on a combination of brand reputation, long-term owner feedback from forums and retailer reviews, and published evaluations from trusted stationery experts. We look for consistent feedback about tip durability (does it hold up after a few fill-ups?), ink behavior (does it bleed through cheap paper or dry out in the cap?), and overall value. We also monitor what actually breaks or disappoints over months of use: tips that mushroom, ink that fades, or caps that crack. By synthesizing this real-world data, we can recommend highlighters that deliver on their promises for the long haul, not just the unboxing moment.
